§ 03 — Method

How we work.

I

Learn your process

Our engineers sit with your subject-matter experts to understand how the work is actually done. We ingest documents, spreadsheets, historical work product, operating procedures, and the systems they live in.

II

Encode your expertise

We convert your firm's institutional knowledge into a custom AI system that performs the work the way your best professionals do — including the judgment calls.

III

Deploy into production

Your system runs on your infrastructure and integrates with your existing workflows and technology stack. No new platform to adopt; no data leaving the building.

IV

Improve every week

Our engineers monitor performance, review exceptions with your team, and continuously sharpen the system. It gets better the longer it runs.
