Case Study: PwC

PwC’s tax teams process some of the most complex financial data on earth—millions of state K‑1 forms containing unstructured tables, handwritten notes, and idiosyncratic state‑specific rules. Traditional automation and off‑the‑shelf AI tools failed here. The work required deep accounting expertise, legacy‑system integration, and rapid iteration across dozens of edge cases.

We embedded our engineers directly alongside PwC’s team. Over the course of weeks, we built and deployed AI agents tailored to their exact process. The agents run invisibly in the background, surfacing only when human input is essential, and pushing final results into the systems PwC accountants already use.
